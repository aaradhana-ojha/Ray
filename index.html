Welcome to Ray

सेवा नई धर्म हो

Our scooter is compatible and easier to control. We are sharing a platform for the mini electric scooters. Our product is based on services we provide to the customer. The main idea of our project is we will be keeping the mini electric scooter in different stations. The customer can get the ride service through the mobile application device. The customer should first search for the scooter near him/her, the location of the scooter is given in the map of the mobile app. After the customer finds the scooter, the customer has to scan the QR code which is placed in the scooter. After scanning the payment option is shown to the customer. The customer should pay according to the desired location he/she wants to reach. The scooter will unlock right after the payment. There will also be subscription models that will help gig workers and others who want to rent for a longer time. Our app has a wallet system which we can load from bank transfer and other wallet systems. We fill the gap of first and last mile.
